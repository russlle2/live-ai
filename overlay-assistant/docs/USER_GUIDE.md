# Sales Coach — User Guide

## What is Sales Coach?

Sales Coach listens to your sales conversations and gives you **word-for-word lines** to say — in real time. Think of it as a friendly co-pilot sitting next to you during every call.

**No rush.** Tips appear when they're relevant, and you can use them, skip them, or ignore them entirely. The system works at your pace.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Open the app
Go to the web URL (default: `http://localhost:5173`).

### 2. Start a session
Click the **Start Session** button. You'll see the status change to "Connected" with a green dot.

### 3. Who's speaking?
Use the speaker toggle to tell the system who's talking:
- **You (Sales Rep)** — when you're speaking
- **The Lead** — when the prospect/client is speaking
- **Not Sure** — if you don't know (the system will still try to help)

This helps the system give you better, more relevant tips.

### 4. Enter what's being said
Type (or paste) what the lead is saying into the text box and press **Enter** or click **Send**.

### 5. Get your tip
A **Quick Tip** card will appear on the right with the exact words you can say. For example:

> **Budget pushback**
> Say: "I totally understand — let's figure out what works for your budget. What range were you thinking?"

### 6. Use or skip
- Click **Used it** if you said the line (this helps us learn what works)
- Click **Skip** if it wasn't relevant
- Click **Tips Paused** to temporarily stop getting tips

---

## Understanding the Tips

### Confidence Levels
Each tip shows how confident the system is:
- ✅ **Strong match** — very likely relevant to what was just said
- 🤔 **Likely relevant** — probably useful, give it a look
- ⚠️ **Just a thought** — might help, skip if it doesn't fit

### "Why this?" button
Click **Why this?** under any tip to see a plain-language explanation of why it was suggested.

## Insights Dashboard

Click the **Insights** tab to see how things are going:
- **Trust Score** — overall system health (0-100)
- **Tips Shown / Used / Skipped** — your engagement stats
- **Errors** — if anything went wrong

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| No tips showing | Make sure you clicked "Start Session" and the status shows "Connected" |
| "Connection lost" message | The app will automatically reconnect. If it doesn't, refresh the page |
| Tips don't match the conversation | Make sure you selected the right speaker (Lead vs. You) |
| Text too small | Use your browser's zoom (Ctrl/Cmd + Plus) |
| Doesn't work on my phone | The app is responsive — try rotating to landscape if the screen is too small |

## Privacy

- **No raw conversation text is stored** — only anonymous hashes for analytics
- All data stays within your team's tenant
- Tips are generated locally from pattern matching, not sent to external AI
